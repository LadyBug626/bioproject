Katheryn Perez

Hey there! I am a sophomore SVAD student and a visual creator, hands-on problem solver, who views the world through a highly structural lens. While a portion of my week is dedicated to studio art classes, my life is driven by a balance of real-world execution and creative discovery. Outside of a traditional classroom setting, I am fascinated by human design, spatial movement, and the timeless arguments of classical philosophy. Right now, my creative goal is to bridge physical anatomy with wearable art, treating every daily routine, logistical challenge, and creative sketch as an opportunity to understand how things are built from the ground up, focusing on costume technology and human design.

Creative Skills

  • Topographical contour and observational drawing
  • Pattern drafting and textile mechanics for garment fabrication
  • Digital collage techniques and visual media experimentation
